About Us

Blockdentity is a digital identity management and KYC platform. Its simplicity offers users a convenient, efficient and secure interaction with multiple service providers, ranging from Government, Health Care, Education and Financial Services. 

Blockdentity ensures sovereign control of identity to users while interacting with various services providers.

Blockdentity is set to revolutionize all forms of transactions via digital identity.
     
    Blockdentity is set to be the World's #1 trusted, secure and allowing users 100% sovereign control of their identity.

    The Blockdentity team is made up of Academics researchers; Consultants; industry thought leaders and professional service providers (Technology, Business strategy, Islamic finance and Operations). We understand the intersection of next generation technology and are focused on simplifying solutions for users around the World.
